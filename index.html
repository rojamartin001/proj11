Welcome

At Bake & Bliss, we serve an inspiring mix of international pastries on small plates designed for sampling and sharing. Drawing from the “food souvenirs” that we have collected during our travels around the world, we have created a menu that reflects everything from the humblest of street foods to the most modern of gastronomic dishes. We invite you to join us and embark on your culinary journey and hopefully take home some new food souvenirs of your!
